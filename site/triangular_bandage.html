Triangular Bandage

Triangular bandages are used to make slings and to make broad bandages to keep splints in place.

Pass around the back of the neck towards the injured side. Make a reef knot(keep knot above the clavicle). Keep corner away from the elbow. Expose all fingers. Twist end, and tuck.
